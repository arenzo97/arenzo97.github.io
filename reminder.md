# Reminder for our wedding!
------------------------------------------------------------

Our wedding is just a few days away, so we wanted to give you some more details. We are so excited that our special day is almost here! Here are a few friendly reminders for **Saturday, 4th April 2026** to help you be prepared for what to expect.

As a reminder for the location, the ceremony and reception are located:

Jubilee Community Centre,

East Grinstead RH19 2HL

## Programme
### 10:00, Arrival
As a reminder for the venue, it can be found below and we ask that you arrive 10:00 as the ceremony starts promptly.


### 11:00, Ceremony
Our Bishop Norman will officiate our church wedding with a short exhortation.


### ~12:00 - 13:30, Canapés and Turnaround
After the ceremony, we ask that guests vacate the main hall as we will reorganise the chairs and tables ready for the lunch and reception. We will provide canapés, some entertainment in the form of an open mic/karaoke, and you’re free to roam around. We just ask you to be back on time so you don’t miss out on lunch!


### 13:30, Lunch and Reception
We will then proceed to our lunch and reception, provided in part by Praise & Plates.
