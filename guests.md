# Guest Attire Guide

We kindly invite our guests to wear colours within the following palette:

- Blue
- Green
- Grey
- Silver

If these colours are not available, **please do not feel pressured to buy new clothing**.  
We warmly encourage you to wear your **best and most comfortable attire**.

Your presence is what matters most to us.
