# Guest Attire Guide

We kindly invite our guests to wear colours within the following palette, reflecting the overall theme of our celebration. As this is a formal event, we kindly request that you wear modest attire for our celebration.

<p align="center">
  <img
    src="/assets/images/palette/guest-attire-palette.png"
    alt="Wedding colour palette in dusty blue and sage green tones"
    style="max-width:320px;"
  />
</p>

The palette above highlights our main theme colours of **dusty blue** and **sage green**.

Guests are welcome to wear attire in shades of:
- Blue
- Green
- Grey
- Silver

If these colours are not available, **please do not feel pressured to buy new clothing**.  
We warmly encourage you to wear your **best and most comfortable attire**.

Your presence is what matters most to us.
